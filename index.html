About the Coin

RugPullCoin was created not just to entertain but to parody the fast-paced and unpredictable world of crypto. While the name suggests chaos, the intention is structure, community and clarity in an otherwise noisy space.

The coin is built on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20), and is currently held by early community wallets. No launch or listing yet.
